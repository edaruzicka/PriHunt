The city of Pripyat

Welcome to the city of Pripyat!

This place has become a ghost town because of the Chernobyl nuclear reactor explosion, that happened on Saturday 26 April 1986.

The whole city had to be evacuated, leaving all of their belonging there. But that's not the most shocking thing that happened back then.

Not only is the Zone around Chernobyl and Pripyat in lock-down, but the local animals have mutated thanks to the high ammount of radiation in the Zone.
